Video Annotations - Content and Format

Please read the instructions below carefully.

In this task, you will be annotating the content and the format for 30 Videos.

  • Content refers to the primary topic of the video.
  • Format refers to the way the video is presented visually.
  • Watch the full video before annotating labels.
  • Carefully select 2 content and 2 format labels that are the most prevelant to the video. In some cases where 2 tags are not applicable, you may choose the same tag for both options within content or format.
  • Use the definitions panel on the right frequently to understand what different options mean.
  • Pick your options based on the most dominant aspects throughout the video
